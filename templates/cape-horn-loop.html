Cape Horn Loop Trail

This 8 mile loop trail will bring you to a great viewpoint over the Columbia at about 1,200 feet then drop you down to the very edge of the river along fields of lava rocks and by basalt towers. You'll pass a waterfall on the way to the Cape Horn Road that will bring you back to the parking lot as well. Tips: Directions to the parking area: Head east on Highway 14 from Vancouver, WA to milepost 26.4 next to Salmon Falls Road. There is no fee to park here. Note: The lower part of the loop below Highway 14 is closed from January 1st to July 1st to protect nesting falcons so plan accordingly.
